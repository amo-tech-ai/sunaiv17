## Purpose

Enable clients to select multiple services their business uses or plans to use. This provides additional context about the client's digital infrastructure and helps the AI make more relevant recommendations in later wizard steps.

**Core Value:** Clients can quickly indicate their service stack, giving the AI better context about their current technology and digital presence. This information enhances industry detection and system recommendations.

---

## Description

Add a multi-select services field to Wizard Screen 1 that allows clients to select from a predefined list of common business services. Clients can select multiple services to indicate what they currently use or plan to implement.

**Available Services:**
- Web Applications
- Mobile Apps
- Chatbots
- AI Agents
- Ecommerce
- Social Media
- WhatsApp

**Selection Behavior:**
- Clients can select multiple services (checkboxes or multi-select dropdown)
- Selections are optional (not required to proceed)
- Selected services are saved with wizard data
- Services inform AI recommendations in later steps

## Implementation

**UI Placement:**
- Add services multi-select field after business description in center panel
- Optional field with clear "Services" label
- Checkbox list or multi-select dropdown
- Visual indication of selected services

**Selection Flow:**
1. User views available services list
2. User selects relevant services (multiple allowed)
3. Selected services are highlighted or checked
4. Selections are saved automatically
5. AI uses selections for context in later steps

**Integration with Screen 1:**
- Services data included in business context
- AI considers services when detecting industry
- Services inform system recommendations in Step 3
- Services help assess digital maturity in Step 4
